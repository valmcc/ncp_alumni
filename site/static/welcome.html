Brief Guide to Adding Content

You can add content by accessing the admin page here: http://ncp.netlify.com/admin and logging in with your email and password.

A few general notes:

  • Try to make sure that images aren't too big (try to keep them under 500 KB)
  • The preview is a bit slow to update (especially with images) - I recommend turning it off (the little eye icon)
  • Try to not post anything too controversial
  • Don't delete/modify other people's contributions - if this does happen I'm making constant backups of every change, so easy to fix
  • Have fun!

    • Eventually when there's enough content on here I'll go about getting a proper website name, etc. - but it's still a work in progress!
